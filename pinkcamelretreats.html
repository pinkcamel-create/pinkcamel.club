Each format is built around a specific kind of transition. Not a programme you join — a structure designed for what you are carrying. The elements are the same. The depth and pace are chosen to fit your moment.

01

Strategic Reset

For founders and leaders between major decisions. The clarity that a familiar environment cannot provide — the Serra offers it freely. Where the next chapter stops being an idea and becomes a direction.

3–6 people · 3 or 6 nights

02

Relocation Prep

Before a move — across cities, countries, continents. The Serra gives you space to arrive at what is actually changing before you enact it. What the body knows about the move before the mind commits.

Solo or duo · 3–10 days

03

Decision Retreat

Before signing, restructuring, announcing, committing. Not a strategy session — a setting where what has been circling finally lands. The decision becomes clear here. It stays clear on return.

Solo · 3–6 nights

04

Executive Rest

For leaders who have been carrying too much for too long. Not a wellness break — a return to the version of yourself that was there before the noise. Deep rest, deliberately structured.

Solo · 3–6 nights

Group & B2B
