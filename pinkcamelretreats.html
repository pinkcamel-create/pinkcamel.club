How you come

Three ways
to come.
The rest is the same.

Every Pink Camel retreat is built on the same foundation: Health, Nutrition, Calm, Mindfulness, and the elements of the land. How you come — alone, with one other person, or with a small group — is the format you choose. The content does not change. Only who you are with.

Private

Solo. One person.

The retreat is built entirely around your situation. What you are carrying. What needs to land. What comes next. No group, no compromise — the full arc of the retreat, shaped entirely around you.

Solo · 3–10 days · Any location

Duo

Duo. Two people.

Two people who need to think together — a couple at an inflection point, two partners, two close colleagues. The retreat is designed for the space between you, and what that space needs to do.

Two people · 3–10 days · Any location

Small Group

Group. Up to 8.

A private group of people who already belong to each other — friends, family, a close circle. The retreat is designed for your specific group, your specific moment. The land holds what ordinary life doesn't.

3–8 people · 3–6 nights · Any location
