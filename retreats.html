Leadership Private Retreats · Pink Camel

Time away,
built around you.

Alone, with one other person, or with a small trusted group. The setting changes. The intention doesn't.

Three ways
to step back.

A retreat isn't a holiday, and it isn't a program. It's deliberate distance from the noise — for a founder who needs to think, a partnership that needs to realign, or a small group that works better away from the office.

We build each one around who's coming and what they need — not a fixed itinerary. Solo, duo, or small group, the setting and the pace are chosen to match.

Formats

Solo

A founder or executive at an inflection point, away from the calendar. Space to think without interruption, structured only as much as you want it to be.

Duo

Two people — co-founders, partners, a parent and a grown child — who need uninterrupted time together, away from the context that usually surrounds them.

Small Group

A trusted circle of four to eight. Leadership teams recalibrating, founders in dialogue, or families gathering with intention. Mornings for conversation, the rest left open.

Our Philosophy

We don't design
escape.

The world has enough places to hide. What's harder to find is a reason to actually be present — with the people who matter, or with your own thoughts.

These retreats exist for people who need a setting designed around them — not around an agenda. The value isn't in what's scheduled. It's in what happens when nothing needs to be performed.

Locations

An orientation, not a menu.

Serra, Portugal. A quiet mountain setting for solo time or a duo. Cork forests, long silences, slow mornings. Chosen when the point is to think, not to perform thinking.

Marrakech & the desert, Morocco. A private riad in the medina, then the open quiet of the desert beyond it. Built for small groups — founders or leadership teams who need distance and each other in the same trip.

The Rhodope Mountains, near Sofia. Close enough to reach easily, remote enough to disappear into. A working retreat for teams and partnerships that need to realign without losing a travel day to get there.
