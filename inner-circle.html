Inner Circle · Pink Camel

For leaders who need
someone in their
corner.

Not a service. A retained relationship with a small number of people whose lives require it.

Not a membership.
A retainer.

The Inner Circle exists because some leaders need someone present across whatever arises — not just the things that can be scheduled, not just the projects that have a defined scope. The things that come up. The decisions that need thinking alongside. The continuity that makes everything else possible.

We work with a deliberately limited number of people at any given time. Not because exclusivity is a selling point. Because the relationship requires it. Because the standard demands it. Because you cannot hold someone's life alongside three dozen other people and do either well.

The terms.

A small number of retainer relationships are held at any given time. The engagement is defined by the relationship, not by a scope of work. We are present when presence is what is needed — across whatever the season requires.

This is not a subscription. It is not a service package. It is a commitment — on both sides.

What continuity
means.

The answer to this question is different for every person in the Circle. What we carry for one leader is not what we carry for another. The retainer is defined by the person, not by a list.

Broadly: the operational weight that would otherwise consume thinking time. The decisions that need a second voice. The transitions that require someone present across them. The things that arise between the things that are already planned.

The thread that runs through all of it: you have someone who understands what you are carrying, who is present across it, and who stays.

"You have someone who understands what you are carrying — who is present across it, and who stays."

The qualifier

Who this
is for.

Inner Circle is for leaders who have already found that episodic or project-based support is not enough. Whose complexity is ongoing. Whose situation changes and requires someone who is already embedded enough to adapt without being briefed from scratch each time.

If this describes where you are, a conversation is the right next step.

If you are looking for a service, Business Concierge or a Retreat is likely the right place to start. The Inner Circle is for people who have already been in that conversation and know they need more.

Begin with a
conversation.

There is no application process. No onboarding form. A conversation first — because we need to understand whether this is the right fit before anything is committed.
