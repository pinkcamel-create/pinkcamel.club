What it means
to be held.

Not everyone needs an Inner Circle. But for those who do — founders scaling across continents, family principals managing real complexity, leaders whose time is the scarcest thing they have — fragmented support has a real cost: missed opportunities, attention spent on the wrong things, decisions made without the full picture.

Inner Circle is a retained advisory relationship. One person who holds your context across every part of life and business — coordinating what no single assistant, consultant, or advisor can hold alone.

This isn't delegation. It's continuity.

What It Includes

A single point of contact who holds your full context — personal, professional, financial, familial.

Priority execution across everything Pink Camel does: transitions, business, experiences.

Strategic presence on the ground, on your behalf, when you can't be there yourself.

Full access to the network we've built across Europe and the Eastern Mediterranean.

Discretion that doesn't need to be written into an agreement, because it's the foundation, not a clause.

What It Isn't

It's not a virtual assistant service. It's not coaching, and it's not therapy. It's not a general concierge that books restaurants and hotels, and it's not administrative overhead for you to manage. It's the relationship that holds everything else together — the thing no single category of service was built to cover.

Philosophy

The opposite of
fragmentation.

The default mode of modern life is fragmentation — one person for your finances, another for your business, another for everything in between. And in the gaps between them, that's where things fall.

Inner Circle exists to close those gaps. Not by adding more advisors — by having one person who sees the whole picture, and coordinates everything else around it.
